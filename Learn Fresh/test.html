Prepare the Chicken
Clean and pat dry the chicken pieces.
Optionally, soak the chicken in buttermilk for a few hours or overnight for tenderness and flavour.
Sprinkle the chicken wings with salt and pepper to taste, ensuring they are evenly seasoned.
Start by cutting the chicken wings into individual sections at the joints to separate.
